About the library

A small, self-published archive built by and for Albuquerque's zine-makers, readers, and misfits.

Our story

Albuquerque Zine Library started as a shelf of traded and donated zines, and grew into a space where anyone can walk in, read for free, and see their own work sitting next to work from across the country. [Add your founding story here.]

What we believe

Self-publishing is for everyone. The collection stays free and open to the public no matter what — memberships exist to keep it that way, not to gate it.

Get involved

Beyond membership, we're always looking for volunteers, donated zines, and people willing to run a workshop. Reach out if that's you.
